This is an easy to make Smoothie

It will only take a couple minutes to make

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 banna
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.
  2. Combine the Ingredients.
  3. Blend until it looks like Smoothie.
