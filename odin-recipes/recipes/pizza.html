Picture of the finished Pizza dough you will make

This is an easy to make Pizza dough

It will only take a couple minutes to make

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Let the dough come to room temperature.
  2. Prepare surface with oil.
  3. Shape the dough.
  4. Put the dough in the oven for 15 minutes.
